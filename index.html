    What Penguins eat!

    Penguins mostly eat krill, squid and fishes. Depending on the species, food preference varies.
    Types of Penguins!
    Emperor King Crested
    Tallest and heaviest penguins. Second largest species of penguins, longest breeding cycle. They have orange hair on top of their head and are native in New Zeland.
